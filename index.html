What it is

A daily feed of books, long-form journalism and true stories matched to your development interests - with rights intelligence attached, so you can move fast on what matters.

Your feed sharpens as you use it. The more your team saves, rejects and comments, the better it understands your slate.

Why it exists

Premium literary scouts charge £30–150K a year. Most independent producers can’t justify that. Low-cost newsletters are static and generic. Rights databases are broad, but they are not built around a producer’s taste, slate and development priorities.

The Ariel sits in the gap - personalised story intelligence at a price smaller teams can actually test.

How it reaches you

A daily email, a living dashboard

Each morning, a curated email digest lands in your inbox with the day’s most relevant stories. Every email links straight to your personalised dashboard - a private web app where you can dig deeper, take action, and train your feed.

The morning email

A concise summary of new stories matched to your brief, designed to scan in under two minutes. Styled to your dashboard’s look and feel - not a generic newsletter. Click any story to open it directly in your dashboard.

Your personalised dashboard

A private web app with your full feed, expanded pitch notes, rights intelligence and “Why Now” context. Use Research to flag stories for your development team, More like this to sharpen your feed, or Archive to clear the noise. Every action teaches your dashboard what you want to see next.
